Gardening

Every year on World Environment Day, interested employees were given potted plants at my work place. Taking care of these plants in my cubicle at work, led me to take up gardening as a hobby at home after I got married. I started with three plants in our balcony. Since then our balcony garden has grown over the years, filled with different flowering plants, money plants, succulents, etc. and our kitchen garden too has several plants like tomato, mint leaves, ginger, turmeric, etc. As our garden grew, so did the number of visitors in our garden :) During the pandemic, since we are home a lot more, my husband and I were lucky to see many of these visits and even capture some of them on camera!
